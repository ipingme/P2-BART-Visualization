BART Data Analytics: Passenger Concentration vs. Time of Day/Station

Purpose:

The purpose of this study was to break down BART commute pattens and to represent these patterns informative visuals.

Research questions:

This study is intended to provide some insights based on the below guiding questions.

  • What is the "busiest" BART station?
  • What time of day is the "busiest" time to commute on BART?
  • When is the best time to commute into San Francisco?

    • Conclusions:

    The below graphs provide information on passenger concentration for a random two-week period in 2016 based on station and time of day.

    Based on the data visualizations, we can conclude that commuting hours (8am-10am and 5-6pm) tend to the most "busy" times (i.e. largest in/out migration per station) on BART. With respect to stations, stations located in the Financia District, which is arguably the center of in/out migration activity in the city are the busiest station based on in/out migration patterns of BART passengers.
